# Landing Page Copy Audit

## Diem Yeu Copy Cu

- Hero con chung chung, chua noi ro DST giup ai va giup viec gi.
- Nhieu CTA lap lai "Lien he tu van", chua noi buoc tiep theo cu the.
- Service card moi mo ta hang muc, chua noi ro viec lam va gia tri khach nhan duoc.
- Section van de con an toan, chua cham vao noi dau nhu fanpage bi bo trong, hinh anh thieu dong bo, ads thieu thong diep.
- Portfolio con nghieng ve mo ta anh, chua noi ro du an thuoc nganh nao va DST ho tro mang gi.

## Nguyen Tac Copy Moi

- Viet theo Pain -> Solution -> Benefit -> Proof -> CTA.
- Cau ngan, ro loi ich, khong pho truong.
- Dung du an that lam proof, khong bia so lieu hay feedback.
- Dinh vi DST la mot dau moi cho website, hinh anh, content, video va quang cao.
- Noi dung phai phu hop tung nganh: nightlife, hospitality, dich vu dia phuong va doanh nghiep can truyen thong so.

## Headline Moi

- Trang chu: "Mot doi ngu cho toan bo hinh anh so cua thuong hieu"
- Bar/Club: "De moi dem dien khong chi dong khach, ma con tro thanh noi dung dang nho"
- Nha hang/Khach san: "Dua trai nghiem nha hang, cafe va khach san len mot hinh anh xung tam hon"
- Dich vu: "Tu website den quang cao, moi diem cham thuong hieu duoc trien khai dong bo hon"

## CTA Moi

- Trang chu: "Nhan tu van goi phu hop", "Xem du an da trien khai"
- Bar/Club: "Xem giai phap Bar/Club", "Tu van chien dich nightlife"
- Nha hang/Khach san: "Xem giai phap Hospitality", "Tu van hinh anh thuong hieu"
- Dich vu: "Kham pha toan bo dich vu", "Nhan tu van goi phu hop"

## Cau Khong Nen Dung Lai

- "Giai phap toi uu hang dau"
- "So 1"
- "Dot pha"
- "Cam ket viral"
- "Cam ket tang doanh thu"
- "Dan dau thi truong"
- "Giup dong khach" neu khong co ngu canh ro rang

## Checklist Truoc Khi Sua Landing Page

- Da xac dinh dung khach hang va muc tieu chinh cua trang.
- Da neu noi dau cu the truoc khi gioi thieu dich vu.
- Da noi ro DST lam gi va khach nhan duoc gi.
- Da dung proof that tu du an, bang gia, ho so nang luc hoac asset san co.
- CTA cu the, khong lap lai may moc.
- Khong thay doi video trong `public/assets/videos`.
- Build/lint pass va text khong tran tren mobile.
