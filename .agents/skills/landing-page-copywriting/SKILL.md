# Landing Page Copywriting

Use this skill when rewriting website, landing page, hero, service, portfolio, proof, or CTA copy.

## Core Formula

Write every major section with this flow:

1. Pain: name the customer's real problem.
2. Solution: explain how DST Group solves it.
3. Benefit: state what becomes clearer, easier, or more professional for the customer.
4. Proof: use real projects, real services, real source-grounded details.
5. CTA: ask for a concrete next step.

## Copy Rules

- Do not invent revenue, reach, conversion, customer count, feedback, or guarantees.
- Do not use claims such as "so 1", "hang dau", "dot pha", "cam ket viral", or "cam ket tang doanh thu".
- Prefer short, direct, landing-page-friendly sentences.
- Write for business owners who need practical marketing support, not for awards judges.
- Use real proof from supplied projects, PDFs, pricing, service data, and assets.
- CTA text must be specific, such as "Nhan tu van goi phu hop", "Trao doi voi DST Group", or "Xem du an da trien khai".

## Page Positioning

- Homepage: DST as one team for website, media, content, video, design, and ads for service brands.
- Bar/Club: event rhythm, line-up, recap, fanpage, nightlife content, and ads.
- Hospitality: food/space visuals, website/fanpage trust, booking/contact intent, clean social content.
- Services: the full ecosystem of website, fanpage, content, media, design, ads, and monthly marketing.

## Safety

- Do not change video files in `public/assets/videos`.
- Do not change images unless asked.
- Do not redesign layout unless asked.
- After copy changes, run build/lint and check mobile/desktop text wrapping.
