# Company Profile Website

Organize the site around what a prospective client needs to evaluate:
- Who the company is.
- What service groups it offers.
- What projects or sectors are shown in source material.
- What pricing or engagement model is explicitly available.
- How to contact the company.

Use client-provided logo, colors, and images. Structure services and case studies from verified data files, not ad hoc component text.

Include an audit trail for all facts and images when the request requires no hallucination.
