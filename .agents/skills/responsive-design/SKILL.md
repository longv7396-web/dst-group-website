# Responsive Design

Design mobile and desktop intentionally rather than relying on shrinkage.

Check:
- Navigation, CTA, and hero content at narrow widths.
- Cards and galleries for equal-height stability and readable wrapping.
- Images for correct aspect ratio, cropping, and loading.
- Tap targets and spacing.
- No horizontal scroll unless intentionally part of a carousel.

Use browser screenshots or viewport checks before final delivery when a runnable app is available.
