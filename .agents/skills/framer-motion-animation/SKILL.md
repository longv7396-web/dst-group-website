# Framer Motion Animation

Use motion to clarify hierarchy and make transitions feel polished. Keep animation subtle.

Preferred patterns:
- Section fade/slide reveal with small travel distance.
- Hover lift or image scale for cards and galleries.
- Staggered child reveals for service grids.
- Respect reduced-motion preferences where practical.

Avoid constant decorative movement that distracts from content. Do not animate layout in ways that cause text overlap or unstable card sizes.
